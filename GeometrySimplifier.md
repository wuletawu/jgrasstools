## Description ##

Collection of geometry semplification algorithms.

This simply sleverages the JTS operations:
  * [DouglasPeuckerSimplifier](http://tsusiatsoftware.net/jts/javadoc/index.html)
  * [TopologyPreservingSimplifier](http://tsusiatsoftware.net/jts/javadoc/index.html)
